Letter Five

A Letter the Distance Will Never Read

Sometimes I think people believe missing someone is about the big moments.
But for me…
It’s always the little things.

I miss your hugs.
I miss the way you’d naturally reach for my hand.
I miss sitting beside you, even if we weren’t saying anything at all.
I miss looking over and knowing you’re right there.

Distance has taught me something I never expected.
It’s possible to miss someone in a hundred tiny ways every single day.

Some days, I wonder how many hugs we’ve had to postpone because of the distance.
How many walks we haven’t taken.
How many quiet moments we could have shared if you were just a little closer.

But then I smile…
Because I know this distance is only temporary.

One day, we’ll get to make up for every hug we’ve missed, every hand we’ve wanted to hold and every moment we’ve had to imagine instead of live.

Until then, I’ll keep looking forward to every hello, holding on to every memory and reminding myself that every day apart is one day closer to being together again.

And I think…
That’s something worth waiting for🩵🧸
